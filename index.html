about me

My name is Gabriel Mousa, I'm a full-time software engineer, and I have fun working on technical things. Currently, my interests lie in ai safety with a particular focus on LLM evals, supervised-fine tuning, rlhf, and any unique ways I can potentially improve upon the post-training process to make models safer for the world. As a whole, I believe AI is inevitable and while there are incredible benefits resulting from it, safety needs to be a core tenant for those building it in order for society to build competently.


Outside of work I'm an avid sailor, cook, swimmer, rock-climber, hiker, and traveller. I live at home with two wonderful cats and most important of all an even more wonderful partner who supports me in all of my endless endeavors. I like to read and try my best to take every moment as a learning opportunity, while leading with curiosity.
